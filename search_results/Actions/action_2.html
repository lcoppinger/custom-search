Keep watching as BEPS actions develop

Base Erosion and Profit Shifting (BEPS)

In September the G20 approved the first set of recommendations from the project on Base Erosion and Profit Shifting (BEPS) run by the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD). Full implementation will not take place until the rest of the project is complete but, having been agreed in principle, business needs to take on board some imminent changes.

The way that BEPS recommendations are implemented by individual territories is crucial to the consistency of international tax rules, the likelihood of double taxation and the level of future tax disputes. In the UK, it will also affect the competitiveness of the tax system.
