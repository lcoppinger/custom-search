EU referendum video reaction - The implications for Scotland

Michael Moore, Senior advisor to PwC gives his views on how Brexit could impact Scotland, whose population voted in favour of remaining in the EU.

Scotland’s First Minister Nicola Sturgeon has today said that a second independence referendum is highly likely and immediately people will want to work out whether or not that means we will now become independent north of the border.

There is an immediate hurdle to get over which is the possibility of a legal referendum; does it need to get Westminster’s permission and agreement while the First Minister thinks it’s inconceivable that Westminster would hold back given the new circumstances in the United Kingdom.
