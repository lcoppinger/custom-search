PwC creates opportunities with Coursera

PwC has joined forces with Coursera, to provide access to a premier course in data analytics

London – Wednesday August 17th, PwC has today announced a collaboration with Coursera, the largest ‘massive open online course’ [MOOC] platform in the world. Coursera is highly respected for the quality of their courses. PwC is amongst one of the first professional services networks to offer courses to their 208,000 employee strong workforce and to the general public on the Coursera platform.

This relationship means that PwC can offer a series of widely available courses for anyone who could benefit from learning more about business analytics. Starting August 22nd, PwC's new “Data Analysis and Presentation Skills​​” course can be taken anytime, anywhere in the world thanks to Coursera's flexible learning platform. This collaboration will also allow PwC’s people to improve their internal learning and development.
