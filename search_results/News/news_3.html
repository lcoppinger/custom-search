PwC shares 10 diversity and inclusion lessons

The launch is part of PwC’s third Global Diversity Week

Sydney, 19 September 2016 – In today’s fast moving world, achieving diversity and inclusion is a critical challenge and opportunity for all organisations around the world. To make progress, diversity must be embedded into the organisation’s DNA – and leadership must be committed to and accountable for diversity.

These are just two of the 10 lessons put forward in a new PwC report – launched today – that looks at how we approach diversity and inclusion and the progress we’ve made.
